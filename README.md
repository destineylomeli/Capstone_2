# Capstone_2 - Sales Territory Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance across the MAryland and Massachusetts territories using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib. The goal of this analysis is to identify sales trends, evaluate performance, compare product categories, and develop recommendations to improve future sales performance.

## Objectives
The project focuses on answering the following business questions:

- Which territories generate the highest sales revenue?
- Which stores perform the best and worst within each territory?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How do sales and transaction counts change over time?
- What opportunities exist for future growth and marketing efforts?

## Tools Used

- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- GitHub

## Analysis Performed

### Question 1: Territory Revenue Analysis
Analyzed total sales revenue by territory and identified overall revenue trends.

### Question 2: Product Category Analysis
Compared product category performance to determine which categories generated the highest and lowest revenue.

### Question 3: Store Performance Rankings
Ranked stores within each territory based on total sales revenue to identify top and bottom performing locations.

### Question 4: Monthly Revenue Trends
Evaluated sales revenue over time to identify growth patterns and seasonal trends.

### Question 5: Transaction Counts and Revenue by Category
Analyzed transaction counts and sales revenue by category to identify the most popular products and opportunities for improvement.

## Visualizations

The notebook includes multiple Matplotlib visualizations that illustrate:

- Revenue by product category
- Transaction counts by category
- Sales trends over time

## Key Findings

- Technology & Accessories generated the highest revenue in both territories.
- Books (General) consistently generated the lowest revenue and transaction counts.
- Several stores significantly outperformed others within their territories.
- Revenue generally increased throughout the analysis period.

## Recommendation

Based on the analysis, the company should continue investing in Technology & Accessories products through increased inventory, promotions, and marketing efforts. Lower-performing categories should be evaluated for additional promotional strategies to improve sales performance.
